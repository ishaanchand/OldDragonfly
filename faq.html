Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change or cancel my order?

BottleTemp aims to dispatch orders within 48 hours. If you need to change or cancel an order please contact us as soon as possible. Once an order has been dispatched we are unable to cancel or change an order, we can however assist with an exchange or return.

I am struggling to place an order. What should I do?

Sometimes computers just don't do what they are told. If you are having trouble completing your purchase you could try opening an private or incognito browser window. If this does not work contact us and we will get the computer experts to do their thing.

How will my items be delivered?

BottleTemp uses Australia Post for all online order deliveries. Once your order has been dispatched you will receive a tracking number that you can use to see the progress of your delivery. To ensure the security of your order you will be required to sign for delivery.

How long will it take to receive my order?

BottleTemp endeavours to dispatch all orders within 48 hours. Once your order has been dispatched you will receive tracking information. From this point, orders are generally received within 2 to 7 business days depending on your location.

Is Bottle Temp environmentally friendly?

Yes, we are! Our focus in the environment and aim to do everything we can to reduce the impact of a climate crisis. Our eco-friendly range is where you can find products that have been made out of stainless-steel materials in an attempt to reduce, reuse and recycle.

I have a general customer query who do I contact?

We would love to hear from you and assist you with any query you have! We have a dedicated team who will aim to get back to you as soon as possible. Feel free to email us at anytime on customer@bottletemp.com.au or call us on 1300 283 670 between 9:00am to 5:00pm Monday to Friday.

How long will my order take to be delivered?

Once you have placed an order you will receive a confirmation email indicating when your order will be ready for shipment. Orders within Australia usually take 3-4 business days to be delivered for, all orders made outside of Australia usually take up to 7-10 business days to be delivered.

How can I customise a water bottle?

Both our Water Bottles and Coffee Mugs have the option to be customised. Once you have picked the product you like you will have the option to customise it with your name or initials, you will also have a range of colours and fonts to pick from as well.

How does the monthly subscription work?

If you opt for our Bottle Temp subscription, customers will receive a powdered sachet containing different flavoured contents that can be combined with either hot or cold liquids. Subscriptions will be billed monthly, and customers can cancel by emailing us anytime on customer@bottletemp.com.au.
