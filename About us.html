About Us

Est. 2022

For us environment is important and is a crucial aspect of our future. We started off with innovating a useful, creative and sustainable line of water bottles and take away coffee mugs to reduce plastic pollution and the impact of a climate crisis.

Our Mission

We started off with innovating a useful, creative and sustainable line of water bottles and take away coffee mugs to reduce plastic pollution and the impact of a climate crisis.

    Currently, we work towards creating healthier lifestyle solutions that will benefit the planet. To make this possible we have created functional products that you’ll want to buy and constantly reuse.

      We want to encourage people to make beneficial sustainable choices everyday so that everyone can work towards a better future.

        Bottle Temp is a company that specialises in reusable water bottles and coffee mugs! it is ideal for everyone wanting to make a positive impact on the environment. We offer several products in a range of different colours and sizes as well as the option for you to customise your products.

          Not only is the environment our focus but we have a strong strive for innovation, that’s why we have designed water bottles and coffee mugs that display the temperature of the liquid inside through a digital gauge.

            Our online business caters for all people, from those plain Jane’s in the world who just want an ordinary water bottle to those with a creative side and want to design their own. We also have an eco-friendly range for your green thumbs out there, as well as an insulated range for those who are on the go 24/7.

              As if that wasn’t enough, we also offer monthly subscriptions for our premium customers where they can sign up and receive sachets of flavoured water and provide feedback on our upcoming products.
